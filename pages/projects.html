  • CKI Discord Bot Github Repository

    • 2021

    A bot service that is used by members of the Circle K International at Sacramento Discord server. Discord is a place for members to talk over voice, video, and text. Our club will use it to make announcments or host online events. My bot provides members with the ability to play music in the voice channel but the main use of the bot is to provide resources and information for members. Users enter a command such as "+hours Torres, Jose" and this will bring up the user's service, leadership, and fellowship hours they have gathered in the club.

    Major technologies used are Python, Wavelink, XLSReader, Discord API, and Spotify API.

  • UTAPS Commute Options Website Github Repository

    • 2020

    A website created for the University Transport and Parking Services. The purpose of the website is to help show student's alternative commute options in order to improve transportation and parking at the university.

    Major technologies used are HTML, CSS, Node.js, and Google Maps API.
