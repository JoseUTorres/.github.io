CKI Discord Bot

CKI Discord Bot was created as a personal project to help out the CKI Sac State Discord server members with doing certain CKI tasks. The bot helps members get resources, information, and view their hours within the club. It also doubles as a music bot and allows users to play the requested music they type in the chat.

CKI Commands

List of commands accessible to the user

  • +hours [lastName], [firstName]
  • +board
  • +calendar
  • +weekly
  • +service
  • +leadership
  • +fellowship
